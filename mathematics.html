Curriculum Overview

Our mathematics curriculum is designed to build strong foundational skills while encouraging critical thinking and logical reasoning:

  • Number Sense: Understanding and working with numbers.
  • Algebra: Introduction to variables, equations, and functions.
  • Geometry: Shapes, spatial reasoning, and measurements.
  • Data Analysis: Learning to interpret and create graphs.
  • Problem Solving: Applying math in real-world contexts.
