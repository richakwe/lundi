Math Olympiad at Lundi Park Primary School

Join the challenge, test your skills, and celebrate the beauty of mathematics!

Event Details

  • Date: March 15, 2025
  • Time: 9:00 AM - 1:00 PM
  • Location: Main Auditorium
  • Eligibility: Open to all students from grades 5 to 12

How to Participate

  1. Register by filling out the participation form by March 1, 2025.
  2. Prepare with our study materials and past papers.
  3. Arrive at the venue with your student ID and necessary writing materials.

Competition Categories

  • Junior Level: Grades 5-8
  • Senior Level: Grades 9-12

Each category will feature problems tailored to the participants' knowledge level in areas like Algebra, Geometry, Number Theory, and Combinatorics.

Prizes

  • 1st Place: Trophy + Gift Card
  • 2nd Place: Medal + Gift Card
  • 3rd Place: Medal + Gift Card
  • Honorable Mentions: Certificates
