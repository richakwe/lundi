Scholarships and Financial Aid

At Lundi Park Primary School, we believe in providing opportunities for all students. Here's information about our scholarship programs:

Types of Scholarships

Academic Excellence Scholarship

Awarded based on academic performance, covering up to 50% of tuition fees.

Need-Based Financial Aid

Designed to assist students with demonstrated financial need.

Sports Scholarship

For students showing exceptional talent in sports, up to full tuition coverage.

Arts Scholarship

Recognizing talent in visual and performing arts, offering partial to full tuition reduction.

Application Process

  1. Complete the General Admission Application
  2. Submit the Scholarship Application Form
  3. Provide necessary documentation (e.g., transcripts, financial statements, portfolios)
  4. Attend an interview or audition if required
  5. Await Scholarship Committee Decision

Scholarship Application Deadlines

  • Early Decision: November 1st
  • Regular Decision: March 1st

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I apply for more than one scholarship?

Yes, you can apply for multiple scholarships, but eligibility criteria must be met for each.

Do scholarships cover all fees?

Scholarships typically cover tuition; other fees like registration or activities might not be included.
