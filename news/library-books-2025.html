New Books in the Library for 2025

February 28, 2025

The Lundi Park Primary School library is excited to announce the addition of a diverse range of new books to our collection. These selections aim to cater to all interests and academic needs of our students, from fiction to science, history to technology, and much more.

Highlights of the New Additions:

  • "Journey Through Time" - An engaging look at history through interactive storytelling.
  • "The Magic of Mathematics" - Making numbers fun with puzzles and games.
  • "Eco-Warriors" - Stories of young activists making a difference in environmental conservation.
  • "Future Tech: What's Next?" - Exploration into the latest technological advancements and their potential impact on society.
  • "Literature Around the World" - A collection of short stories from various cultures, promoting global understanding.

We've also expanded our digital resources, offering new titles through our e-library platform, ensuring that students can access these books anytime, anywhere.

This initiative is part of our commitment to fostering a love for reading and learning. We encourage all students to visit the library, explore these new books, and perhaps find a new favorite. Our librarians are always ready to help you find the perfect read or assist with research projects.

Don't forget to check out our new reading nooks designed for comfort and inspiration!
