Science Fair 2025 Winners Announced

March 15, 2025

We are thrilled to announce the winners of this year's Science Fair at Lundi Park Primary School. The competition was fierce, with students from all grades showcasing their creativity and scientific understanding. Here are the highlights:

First Place

John Smith - Grade 10 - Project: "Solar Powered Water Purification System"

John's project not only won first place but also demonstrated a practical application of solar energy in solving real-world problems.

Second Place

Emily Johnson - Grade 8 - Project: "The Effects of Music on Plant Growth"

Emily's research into how different types of music affect plant growth was both intriguing and well-executed.

Third Place

Alex Rivera - Grade 12 - Project: "AI for Predicting Weather Patterns"

Alex's use of artificial intelligence to predict weather patterns was both innovative and insightful, earning him third place.

Honorable mentions went to numerous students for their outstanding efforts in various scientific fields. The Science Fair was not just about competition but also about sharing knowledge, inspiring innovation, and fostering a love for science among all participants.

We want to thank our judges, sponsors, and all the students who participated. The creativity and dedication shown by our students are what make our school community proud. Stay tuned for more events and opportunities to explore the wonders of science at Lundi Park Primary School.
