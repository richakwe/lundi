Our Curriculum

At Lundi Park Primary School, we offer a comprehensive curriculum designed to foster well-rounded development, critical thinking, and a love for learning.

Curriculum Overview

Our curriculum is structured to meet national educational standards while encouraging creativity, innovation, and personal growth:

  • Core Subjects: Mathematics, Science, English Language Arts, Social Studies.
  • Arts and Humanities: Visual Arts, Music, Drama, World Languages.
  • Physical Education and Health: Sports, Wellness Education, Nutrition.
  • Technology and Media: Computer Science, Digital Literacy.
  • Special Programs: STEM, Environmental Education, Community Service.

Curriculum by Grade

Grade 1 focuses on basic literacy, numeracy, social skills, and introduces students to:

  • Reading and Phonics
  • Basic Math Operations
  • Introduction to Science
  • Art and Music Exploration
